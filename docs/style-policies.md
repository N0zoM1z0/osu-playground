# Style Policy Guide

## Built-In Packs

- `flow_aim`
- `jump_control`
- `farm_jump`
- `stream_focus`
- `deathstream`
- `hybrid_tech_light`

## What a Policy Pack Controls

Each pack defines:

- density policy
- rhythm simplification
- note-selection bias
- spacing schedule
- angle smoothness / volatility
- slider ratio and connector bias
- phrase continuity
- chorus lift
- strain choreography
- repetition and rest policy
- hitsound emphasis
- SV hooks
- candidate ranking weights

## Prompt Resolution

Prompt parsing maps natural language into:

- canonical tags
- policy mixtures
- explicit constraints
- negative constraints

Examples:

- `flow aim`
- `jump map with easy reading`
- `farm jump with chorus lift`
- `deathstream but not too tech`
- `melodic flow aim, low slider spam`

## Reference Fusion

When references are supplied, the merged policy is nudged by the reference profile:

- jump-heavy references increase base spacing
- flow-heavy references increase slider ratio
- stream-heavy references raise density policy
